HARDWARE

This is an image of the laptop before my partner and I took it apart.

This is the keyboard of the laptop. The keyboard is the buttons where there
are letters and when you press on them whatever you type will show on the screen.
The keyboard helps you by providing the letters so you can type whatever you need
to start/finish a project or an essay.

This is the mouse of the laptop. The mouse is the the the square on the bottom
of the laptop. You move your finger to move the arrow on the screen
so you can press any link on the screen. Not only it s to press any link on the
screen, also it allows you to move your arrow that is on the screen any where you want.

This is the monitor. I call this the screen, but the sophisticated way to call it is monitor.
The monitor is the screen. This part is where you see everything you do. For example, you see what
you're typing and where you are dragging your the arrow that you are moving from your mouse.

This is the motherboard. What is a motherboard?, you may ask. Well a motherboard is a
circuit where it contains principal components of a device, in this case a laptop,
where there are connectors and other ciruit boards can be connected. A motherboard
has connecters and with those you can connect different ciurcuit boards so every other piece
of the device can work.

This is the CPU. CPU stands for Central Processing Unit and this piece
is the brain of the laptop. This amazing piece follows all the directions
you give your computer.

This other piece is called a RAM. No, this is not a male sheep, it stands for something
too. RAM stands for Random Access Memory. This piece is a hardware device. This
hardware device allows all of the information to be stored and recuperate when needed.
Usually the information gets accessed randomly rather than constantly like it is on a disk,
the computer can approach it faster.

This last pice is called a hard drive. This small piece is a self contained mini storage.
It contains a read and write structure that has one or two hard cd's that are inside
a shut section of the drive.

All these parts communicate with each other, but how? The main part is the motherboard. What is connected to the motherboard is the CPU. In the CPU you can add a memory and other cards to it. Also, connected to the motherboard is the RAM. It is located bellow the CPU. The hard drive is not connected with the motherboard but its above the motherboard.
