About Vindinum

Vindinium is a game where 4 players, "heros", play in a field and their goal is to be the one who wins the land of vindinium. This
game is an Artificial Intelligence progamming challenge. To achieve the goal to own the land vindinium you need to collect the most gold
you can. You can program your robot to attack others to gain gold and mines. You program your robot to go to a tavern to drink their gold!
